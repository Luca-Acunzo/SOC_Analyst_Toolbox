# Reports
- **Save As:** Use this feature to save a copy of your report with a new name.
- **Reports Tab:** In this tab, you can view all your saved reports.
- **View Details:** Click on a report to see its specific configurations and metadata.
- **Scheduling:** You can automate your reports by scheduling them to run at specific intervals.
